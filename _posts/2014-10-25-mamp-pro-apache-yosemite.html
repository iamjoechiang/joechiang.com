I recently upgraded my MacBook Pro to OS X Yosemite and MAMP Pro (I'm running version 2.1.4) – Apache won't start after the upgrade.

Here is a quick fix.

  1. Navigate to /Applications/MAMP/bin/apache2/bin in your Finder
  2. Rename the file envvars to _envvars
  3. Start MAMP Pro

Done!

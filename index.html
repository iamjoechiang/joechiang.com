What I do

Design

I create beautiful, accessible websites that engage your target audience, add value to your business and are easy to use. As most website traffic comes via a mobile device, I specialise in Responsive Web Design and have a ‘Mobile First’ approach that ensures your website looks great and loads fast on a variety of devices at all resolutions.

Development

I use open source based content management systems including WordPress, Craft and Siteleaf to develop and build websites with the latest web standards such as HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. Features and software depend on the size, scale and budget of your website or project.

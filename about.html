Hi, I am Joe. I live in Perth and work in Web Development at Curtin University, Western Australia

Perth

This is my portfolio of work and my Journal on travel and technology.

I enjoy working with people to create websites that are easy and intuitive to use. Simplicity and Clarity are my two main goals when designing websites and user interfaces. I am more interested in giving you a solution to having an online presence that works for you and your customers and clients than the option that would make me the most money.

So if you have a project in mind - I would love to help you out. Please email me at hello@joechiang.com.au

Background

I was born in Taiwan and moved to Japan when I was nine. After university, I worked as a Software Engineer for two years before migrating to Australia.

I am not always in front of a computer – I also enjoy playing beach volleyball and will hopefully learn surfing this summer.
